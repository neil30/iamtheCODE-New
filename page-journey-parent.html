The iamtheCODE Parents Orientation held on February 19, 2025, at the JHS Computer Laboratory brought together parents and guardians for an engaging introduction to the program’s goals and opportunities. The session highlighted how iamtheCODE empowers learners through coding, creativity, and digital skills, while also emphasizing the important role of parents in supporting their children’s journey. The orientation fostered awareness, collaboration, and excitement as families learned how the program will shape students’ future readiness in the digital world.
