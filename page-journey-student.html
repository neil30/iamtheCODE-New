Student Orientation

The orientation of THS student participants for the Pilot Implementation of Project iamtheCODE was held on February 18, 2025, at the Taal High School Covered Court. The event introduced students to the program’s goals, structure, and the opportunities it offers in coding, digital literacy, and STEAM learning. Learners were also oriented on the 12-module journey that will guide their growth as young innovators. The activity highlighted the importance of creativity, problem-solving, and collaboration as students begin their iamtheCODE experience. With the support of school leaders and facilitators, the initiative aims to empower learners to build confidence, develop future-ready skills, and become active contributors to digital innovation.
